Biryani Recepie

Ingredients are:--

  • Basmati Rice
  • Water
  • Biryani Spices
  • Paneer
  • Salt
  • Chopped Vegetables

Cooking Process:--

Instructions:--

  1. Soak Basmati Rice for 1/2 hr.
  2. Roast the Biryani Spices in oil until the raw flavour is lost.
  3. Roast the Chopped vegetables until the rawness is clear.
  4. Mix the chopped vegetables with soaked rice and cook for 1/2hr in dim flame.
That's it! Tasty Biryani is ready and it's ready to serve...!
